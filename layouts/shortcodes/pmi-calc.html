At 20% equity you can request PMI removal. At 22% equity (78% LTV) your lender must cancel it automatically under the Homeowners Protection Act. PMI rates vary 0.2–2% annually — check your loan documents for your exact rate. FHA MIP rules differ from conventional PMI.
